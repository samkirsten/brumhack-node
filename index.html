Welcome to

Phone Gladiator!

Phone Gladiator is a game of agility, speed and endurance that you can play with your friends! All you need to play are two smartphones and an internet connection.

Rules

At least two phones must be placed at a minimum of 5 metres apart

Players must start by their phones and attempt to touch their opponent's phone

A round is over once all but one phone has been touched

If there are more than two players then it is a free-for-all.

The winner is the person who's phone has remained untouched!

We don't take responsibility for any broken devices..!
